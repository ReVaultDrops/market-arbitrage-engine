# Revault Premium Guide to Sneaker Reselling

## Executive Summary
Revault treats sneaker reselling as a **systems business**, not a hype business. The global market is projected to reach $137.6B by 2033. Revault promises disciplined sourcing, trusted authentication, and repeatable profit math.

## Market Landscape
- **Primary Market**: $104.5B in 2025.
- **Resale Layer**: $10.6B in 2025, projected to $27.7B by 2035.
- **Demographics**: 60% of Gen Z males use StockX; 33% consider themselves sneakerheads.

## Hybrid Channel Model
- **eBay**: Demand engine, sneaker sold every 5s. Best for broad liquidity and used inventory.
- **StockX**: 60M lifetime trades. Data-rich for DS/New, SKU-exact trading.
- **GOAT**: 60M members. Best for used pairs, consignment, and high-AOV inventory.
- **Shopify**: Margin-maximizing DTC layer. Own the customer relationship and trust stack.

## Product Strategy: Barbell Portfolio
- **Liquidity First (Core)**: Samba, Gel-1130, Jordan 3/4/11, NB 9060, Air Max, SB Dunk.
- **Rarity Second (Momentum)**: Travis Scott, Kobe retros, designer collabs, ASICS Gel-Kayano 14.

## Sourcing Architecture
- **Retail Drops**: SNKRS, CONFIRMED. Focus on account hygiene and device readiness over bots.
- **Liquidation**: B-Stock for volume, returns, and overstock directly from retailers.
- **Marketplace Sourcing**: Comp discipline via eBay Product Research (3 years of data) and StockX Pro.
- **International**: Customs/Duty awareness. Post-Aug 2025 rules suspend duty-free treatment for many shipments under $800.

## Authentication & Risk
- **eBay Authenticity Guarantee**: Free for eligible $100+ pairs; 2-day inspection.
- **StockX Verified**: Requires brand-new with original box. $15 non-compliance penalty.
- **GOAT**: Supports new and used. Requires photos for used/defects.
- **Risk Reserves**: Maintain reserves for authentication failure, returns, and shipping loss.

## Pricing & Profit Math
- **Fee Benchmarks**: eBay (~8% for $150+), StockX (3% processing + 7-9% transaction), GOAT (9.5% + seller fee).
- **Calculator Logic**: Net Profit = Sale Price - (Purchase Cost + Inbound + Platform Fees + Shipping + Packaging + Reserve).

## Operations & Compliance
- **Tech Stack**: Shopify + ShipStation + Airtable + StockX Pro.
- **Legal**: LLC for liability protection.
- **Tax**: 1099-K threshold ($20k/200 transactions). Marketplaces remit sales tax in most US jurisdictions.
- **IP**: VeRO compliance. Use original photography; avoid implying official brand affiliation.

## 90-Day Action Plan
- **Days 1-30**: Entity setup, resale certificate, marketplace accounts, inventory base.
- **Days 31-60**: First 20-40 listings, shipping SOP, authentication checklist, comp routine.
- **Days 61-90**: Category optimization, margin review, SOP standardization, supplier scorecarding.
